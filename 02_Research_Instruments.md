# Research Instruments
## E20 Petrol Adoption — Survey & Interview Guides

---

## A. Consumer Survey Questionnaire (48 Questions)

**Instructions to enumerator:** Administer at petrol pumps (intercept) and via online panel. Estimated completion time: 10-12 minutes. Screen respondent must own/primarily drive a petrol 2W or 4W.

### Section 1: Screener & Demographics (Q1-Q8)
1. Do you own or primarily drive a petrol vehicle? (Yes / No — terminate if No)
2. Vehicle type: 2-Wheeler / 4-Wheeler / Both
3. Vehicle manufacture year: Before 2018 / 2018-2022 / 2023 or later / Not sure
4. Vehicle brand and model (open text)
5. City / Town of residence
6. City tier: Metro / Tier-2 / Tier-3 / Rural (enumerator-coded)
7. Age group: 18-25 / 26-35 / 36-45 / 46-60 / 60+
8. Gender: Male / Female / Prefer not to say / Other

### Section 2: Awareness (Q9-Q16)
9. Have you heard of "E20" petrol? (Yes / No)
10. Where did you first hear about E20? (Petrol pump signage / News/TV / Social media / Dealer/showroom / Friends/family / Government advertisement / Other)
11. In your own words, what do you think E20 means? (open text — coded later for accuracy)
12. On a scale of 1-5, how well do you understand what E20 petrol is? (1=Not at all, 5=Very well)
13. Are you aware E20 is now sold at most petrol pumps in India? (Yes / No / Not sure)
14. Do you know the current ethanol blending percentage in regular petrol? (0% / 10% / 20% / Don't know)
15. Have you seen E20 signage at the pump you visit most often? (Yes / No / Not sure)
16. Has anyone at a petrol pump ever explained E20 to you verbally? (Yes / No)

### Section 3: Adoption Behavior (Q17-Q24)
17. Have you knowingly purchased E20 petrol? (Yes / No / Not sure)
18. Do you actively try to avoid E20 petrol? (Yes, always / Sometimes / No, never / Not aware I could)
19. If you avoid it, why? (Mileage concerns / Engine damage concerns / Warranty concerns / Habit/no reason / Not applicable)
20. How often do you check the fuel label/pump before refueling? (Always / Sometimes / Rarely / Never)
21. Would you switch pumps to avoid E20 if a non-E20 option were available nearby? (Yes / No / Not sure)
22. Has your vehicle manual or dealer ever mentioned E20 compatibility? (Yes / No / Don't recall)
23. Did you check E20 compatibility before/after your last vehicle purchase? (Yes, before / Yes, after / No)
24. How many petrol refills have you done in the last month? (numeric)

### Section 4: Perceived Impact (Q25-Q34)
25. Have you noticed any change in mileage since E20 was introduced? (Improved / No change / Slightly worse / Significantly worse / Not sure)
26. If worse, by approximately what percentage? (0-5% / 5-10% / 10-20% / 20%+ / Not measured, just a feeling)
27. Have you experienced any engine performance issues you attribute to E20? (Yes / No / Not sure)
28. Have you experienced any starting/idling issues you attribute to E20? (Yes / No / Not sure)
29. Have you had to visit a service center for an issue you believe is E20-related? (Yes / No)
30. Rate your concern about engine damage from E20 (1=Not concerned, 5=Very concerned)
31. Rate your concern about mileage loss from E20 (1=Not concerned, 5=Very concerned)
32. Rate your concern about warranty voidance from E20 (1=Not concerned, 5=Very concerned)
33. Do you believe E20 is safe for vehicles manufactured before 2023? (Yes / No / Not sure)
34. Do you believe your specific vehicle is E20-compatible? (Yes / No / Not sure)

### Section 5: Trust & Communication (Q35-Q40)
35. How much do you trust the government's messaging on E20? (1-5 scale)
36. How much do you trust your vehicle manufacturer (OEM)'s messaging on E20? (1-5 scale)
37. How much do you trust the petrol pump/dealer's messaging on E20? (1-5 scale)
38. How much do you trust social media/word-of-mouth information on E20? (1-5 scale)
39. What source would you trust MOST for accurate E20 information? (Government / OEM / Dealer / Independent mechanic / Social media / News media)
40. Would a "verified E20-compatible" sticker on your vehicle increase your confidence? (Yes / No / Not sure)

### Section 6: Price & Incentive Sensitivity (Q41-Q44)
41. Are you aware of any price difference between E20 and pure petrol at your pump? (Yes / No)
42. Would a price discount on E20 increase your willingness to use it? (Yes / No / Already willing)
43. Would a loyalty/rewards program for E20 usage change your behavior? (Yes / No / Not sure)
44. Would a government incentive to upgrade an incompatible vehicle influence your decision? (Yes / No / Not applicable, my vehicle is compatible)

### Section 7: Overall Sentiment & Open Feedback (Q45-Q48)
45. Overall, how do you feel about India's shift to E20 petrol? (Very positive / Somewhat positive / Neutral / Somewhat negative / Very negative)
46. Net Promoter-style: How likely are you to recommend that a friend not worry about using E20? (0-10 scale)
47. What is the ONE thing that would most increase your confidence in E20? (open text)
48. Any additional comments? (open text)

*Question design notes: Q11, Q47, Q48 are open-text for qualitative coding. Q12/Q30-32/Q35-38/Q46 form Likert/NPS-style scales used for the statistical and dashboard sections. Q3 + Q25-29 are the primary inputs for H2/H3 hypothesis testing.*

## B. Consumer In-Depth Interview (IDI) Guide (12-15 interviews, 20-30 min each)

**Objective:** Uncover the emotional and reasoning layer behind survey numbers — the "why" behind adoption or resistance.

**Warm-up**
- Tell me about your vehicle and how you use it day to day.
- How long have you been driving, and how often do you refuel?

**Awareness & First Exposure**
- When did you first hear about E20? Walk me through that moment.
- What was your first reaction?

**Understanding & Beliefs**
- In your own words, what do you believe E20 does to a vehicle?
- Where did that belief come from? Has anything ever changed your mind?

**Experience**
- Have you noticed anything different about your vehicle's performance recently? Tell me about a specific incident, if any.
- Would you say that's definitely because of E20, or could there be other explanations?

**Trust & Information**
- If you had a question about E20 right now, who would you ask first? Why them?
- Do you trust what the petrol pump tells you? Why or why not?

**Decision-Making**
- Walk me through your thought process the last time you refueled — did E20 cross your mind at all?
- What would it take for you to feel completely confident about E20?

**Closing**
- If you were advising the government on how to explain E20 better, what's the one thing you'd tell them?

## C. Dealer / Franchisee Interview Guide (8-10 interviews, 30-40 min each)

**Target:** Petrol pump owners/franchise operators (distinct from pump attendants — see Guide D).

**Business Context**
- How long have you operated this outlet, and under which OMC brand?
- How has fuel demand/mix shifted since E20 rollout began?

**Operational Impact**
- What training or materials did you receive from the OMC about E20?
- How do your staff currently explain E20 to customers — is there a standard approach?
- What is your estimate of how many customers ask questions about E20 per day/week?

**Customer Sentiment (Dealer's View)**
- What are the most common complaints or concerns customers raise?
- Have you seen any change in customer loyalty or pump-switching behavior since E20?
- Do you think customers understand what they're being sold?

**Commercial Impact**
- Has E20 affected your sales volume, margins, or complaint-handling costs?
- Have you had any disputes or escalations related to E20?

**Suggestions**
- What support from the OMC or government would make your job easier here?
- If you could change one thing about how E20 is communicated, what would it be?

---

## D. Petrol Pump Attendant / Frontline Staff Interview Guide (8-10 interviews, ~20 min each)

**Target:** Frontline attendants who interact directly with customers at the dispenser.

**Role & Routine**
- Walk me through a typical fuel transaction — what do you say, if anything, about the fuel grade?
- Do customers usually ask what fuel they're getting, or do they not ask at all?

**Knowledge & Training**
- What were you told about E20 during your training, if anything?
- If a customer asked you right now "will E20 damage my bike," how would you answer?

**Customer Interactions**
- What's the most common question or complaint you hear about E20?
- Have you ever had an upset or angry customer because of E20? What happened?

**Barriers**
- Is there anything stopping you from explaining E20 to every customer (time pressure, lack of script, not confident in the answer)?
- Do you have any printed material or signage you can point customers to?

**Suggestions**
- What would make it easier for you to explain E20 confidently?
